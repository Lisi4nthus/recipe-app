The design system currently uses a `light` color mode.



### Color Palette

- **Primary Color:** `#4CAF50` (A vibrant green, suggesting freshness and growth)

- **Secondary Color:** `#FF9800` (A warm orange, offering a friendly contrast)

- **Neutral Color:** `#F9F9F9` (A very light grey, providing a clean and subtle background)



### Typography

- **Headlines:** `plusJakartaSans` (Modern and clean for headings)

- **Body Text:** `inter` (Readable and versatile for paragraphs and longer texts)

- **Labels:** `inter` (Consistent with body text for form labels and small annotations)



### Shape

- **Roundedness:** `2` (Moderate roundedness, offering a balanced and friendly aesthetic to UI elements)



### Spacing

- **Spacing:** `2` (Normal spacing, ensuring a good balance between density and readability)
